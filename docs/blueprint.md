# **App Name**: SalonEase

## Core Features:

- Google Sign-In: Allow users to log in using their Google accounts.
- Appointment Scheduling: Enable customers to book appointments online, selecting service, date, and time.
- Service Catalog: Display available services with descriptions, prices, and duration.
- Stylist Profiles: Showcase stylists with their bios, specialties, and photos.
- Automated Reminders: Send automated appointment reminders via email or SMS.
- Review and Rating System: Allow customers to leave reviews and ratings for services and stylists.
- Personalized Recommendation: Suggest relevant services to the customer based on service history using generative AI tool. The model has access to service catalogs, and stylist specialties

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Muted rose (#D8B4A0) for a welcoming, comforting atmosphere.
- Background color: Soft beige (#F5F5DC) provides a clean and calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Deep brown (#8B4513) used sparingly for calls to action and important details.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a humanist serif font to bring an elegant and intellectual contemporary feel.
- Use elegant line icons for services and features.
- Clean and spacious layout to give a sense of calm and luxury.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience.
